ARXIV SUBMISSION NOTES
=============

  main.tex
  references.bib
  figures/
    *.pdf
  anc/
    (all these files)

Submission format:
  tar.gz or zip containing main paper + anc/ directory

Size limits:
  Main submission: <50 MB
  Ancillary files: <500 MB total
  Individual files: <200 MB
